About Akili Nest

Who We Are

Akili Nest is a Nairobi-based STEM education provider making world-class coding, robotics and AI accessible to every young Kenyan.

Our programs are powered by PictoBlox, Quarky Robotics and Python. Every course ends with a real project students are proud to show the world.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya. We also partner directly with schools across the city.
Globally connected. Students enter international competitions and earn globally recognised certificates.
